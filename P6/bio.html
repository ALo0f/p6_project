Tianyi Wu

My name is Tianyi Wu. I am currently pursuing a Master Degree in Computer Science at Northwestern University.
The four-year undergraduate and the ongoing graduate education in Computer Science helped me to build a solid theoretical foundation and develop practical hands-on skills for my future career in technology. During my recent internship at Amazon CDK team, I created and designed an API providing easy data encryption for Global Tables in DynamoDB and reducing Global Table creation code by 50%. Additionally, I implemented github actions that could encourage more open source contributions from community contributors outside of the Amazon CDK team. My engineering skills together with an entrepreneurial mindset always empower me to solve problems, overcome challenges, and take full ownership in my personal and professional life.
